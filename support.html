Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Q How do I reset my password?
Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen and we'll send a reset link to your email.
Q How do I delete my account?
To delete your account and all associated data, please email us at hello@cleohistory.com with the subject "Delete Account".
Q The app is crashing — what should I do?
Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, please email us at hello@cleohistory.com with details of your device and what you were doing when the crash occurred.
Q How do I earn profile pictures?
Some profile pictures are unlocked by completing the Grand Trial for a topic. Look for the lock icon to see what's required.
Q Why am I seeing ads?
Cleo is free to download and comes with a generous daily ad-free allowance — each day you get 3 modules and 3 quizzes completely free with no ads. Ads only appear once you've used your daily free quota, and they help us keep the app free for everyone. You can opt out of personalised ads at any time in your device settings.
